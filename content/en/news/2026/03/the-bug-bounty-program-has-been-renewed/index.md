The FreeCAD Project Association has renewed the bug bounty program it launched last year. The budget is once again €5,000.

The rules are simple: fix 5 confirmed issues from the FreeCAD GitHub issue tracker and receive a reward of €250.

For a quick guide on finding the issues and claiming the reward after getting the fixes merged, please see the [original announcement](https://blog.freecad.org/2025/08/14/pilot-bugfix-rewards-program/).

The previous fund was distributed in twelve payouts to seven FreeCAD contributors: matthiasdanner, tetektoza, wwmayer, ipatch, YashSuthar983, davidgilkaufman, and Roy-043.
