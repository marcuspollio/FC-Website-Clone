After an enormous amount of work and dedication from FreeCAD contributors we are delighted to announce that FreeCAD Version 1.1 is now released and available for [download](https://www.freecad.org/downloads).

There are significant amounts of improvements and new features. These include; transparent Part Design previews, interactive draggers added to tools like Fillet and Chamfer, 3 point lighting, a Clarify Selection tool, Assembly and FEM improvements and animations, a totally new CAM tool library system and much much more.

For a full list of changes and new features check out the [Release Notes](https://wiki.freecad.org/Release_notes_1.1) and if you want to support the ongoing development of FreeCAD then do consider making a [donation](https://wiki.freecad.org/Donate)!
