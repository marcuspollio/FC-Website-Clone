We've just done the first payouts in the pilot bugfix rewards program launched in August. Two contributors have claimed the reward so far: tetektoza fixed 15 confirmed issues, and matthiasdanner fixed another five. Congrats!

We have more money to spend on this, so if you already qualify for a reward or just thinking about joining, please see [this blog post](https://blog.freecad.org/2025/08/14/pilot-bugfix-rewards-program/) for details about the program. Now that we are fully in bugfix mode for v1.1, it's as good time as any to contribute!
