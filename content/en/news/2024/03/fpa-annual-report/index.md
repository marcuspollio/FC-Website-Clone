The FreeCAD project association is proud to present its annual report of the year 2023, and its plans for 2024. Find the report here:

[https://fpa.freecad.org/annualreports/2024.html](https://fpa.freecad.org/annualreports/2024.html)

The FreeCAD project association or, as we like to call it, the FPA, is the non-profit body behind the FreeCAD project. "Behind" is not exactly the right term here, because it was specially designed to be a tool for the FreeCAD community, not a governing body. The FPA is responsible for collecting and spending the money donated to FreeCAD, and to help it reach the people who need it.
