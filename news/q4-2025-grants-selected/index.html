Q4 2025 grants selected

December 25, 2025 Aleksandr Prokudin (169)Announcement (94)FPA (44)Grant (22)1 min

The FPA members have voted for Q4 2025 grants.

tetektoza will revamp and enhance the selection system around specific bug reports listed in the grant application. He will be awarded a grant of 3,000 EUR.

Pierre-Louis Boyer aka PaddleStroke received two grants, for maintaining and improving the Assembly and Sketcher workbenches, 3,000€ for each grant. Work on Assembly will include both new features and bugfixes, work on Sketcher will be focused primarily on bugfixes.

Morgan Allen received a grant to get his KiConnect PCB to the first public release and then enhance its functionality. The workbench he’s been working on provides bidirectional interoperability between FreeCAD and KiCADs v9+ API. Morgan will be awarded a grant of $5200 for the project.

Additionally, Aleksandr Prokudin will update Ondsel Lens documentation to cover recently added features and write documentation for the Lens addon. Aleksandr will be awarded a grant of 1,000 EUR from the separate Ondsel Onward fund.

We’ll be introducing substantial changes to the 2026 grant program. Stay tuned for updates!
