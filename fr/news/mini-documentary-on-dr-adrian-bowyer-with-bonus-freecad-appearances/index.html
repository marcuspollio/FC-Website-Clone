Mini Documentary on Dr Adrian Bowyer, with bonus FreeCAD appearances!

5 janvier 2026 Jo Hinchliffe (77)Showcase (30)User Story (22)Documentary (1)1 min

Spotted in the wild is this excellent mini documentary with Dr Adrian Bowyer. If the name is unfamiliar, Dr Adrian Bowyer founded the opensource RepRap project with the idea of self replicating 3D printers. The RepRap project has a very real claim that it was the catalyst and driver that led to many of us have 3D printers in our homes and workshops.

In this great documentary we hear this story, whilst also touring Dr Bowyer’s amazing home research/lab/engineering space. It’s delightful that from around the 6 minute 30 second mark we see Dr Bowyer sat in front of a large computer monitor with a wonderful CAD project open in FreeCAD!
