WIP Wednesday - 27 Dec 2023

27 décembre 2023 Aleksandr Prokudin (169)Update (158)WIP (153)2 min

This week in FreeCAD development:

  • abdullahtahiriyo activated new icon for optional unified coincident constraint.
  • FlachyJoe fixed a display error with the point-to-line distance constraint.
  • maxwxyz did an icon overhaul for the Sketcher workbench to unify the appearance and adjusted cursor icons to match toolbar icons.
  • WandererFan merged several fixes for the TechDraw workbench.
  • pavltom implemented proper selection of subitems and refactored tree view updates in TechDraw.

PR stats: In the week from Wednesday, 20 Dec to Wednesday, 27 Dec there were 15 pull requests merged, with a total codebase change of +623 / -327 (not counting all icon changes). We had an average merged PR size of +48 / ‑25. 20 new pull requests were opened.

Issue stats: we closed 24 issues, and 13 new issues were opened. Overall, 1,099 issues are currently open.

The FreeCAD Project Association recently approved three new members:

  • Stephen Hawes, CEO and founder of Opulo, creators of the LumenPnP desktop pick-and-place machine designed with FreeCAD.
  • Paulo Kiefe, founder of Stemfie, a free & open-source 3D-printable and open-sourced educational construction set.
  • Shai Seger, lead developer of Fasteners and Sheet Metal workbenches.

Extending the general assembly to include founders of FreeCAD-powered businesses and educational projects, as well as developers of popular workbenches will hopefully bring new perspectives to the project.
